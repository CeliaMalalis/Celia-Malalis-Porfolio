Celia's Delicious Bicol Express Recipe

Ingredients

  • Meat: 1 kg pork belly, cut into small 1-inch cubes
  • Aromatics: 1 large red onion (chopped), 6-8 cloves garlic (minced), 2-inch ginger (minced)
  • Chili: 10-15 pcs siling haba (long green chilies), sliced
  • Spiciness: 5-10 pcs Thai chili peppers (optional, for extra heat)
  • Sauce: 2 cups coconut milk & 1 cup coconut cream
  • Flavor: 2-3 tbsp shrimp paste (bagoong alamang)
  • Cooking Oil: 2 tbsp (for sauteing)
  • Seasoning: Salt and ground black pepper to taste

Procedure

  1. Prepare the Pork: In a wide pan or wok, sear the pork belly until lightly browned and some fat is rendered.
  2. Saute Aromatics: Add garlic, onion, and ginger. Saute until aromatic and softened.
  3. Add Shrimp Paste: Add the shrimp paste (bagoong) and cook for 1-2 minutes.
  4. Simmer in Coconut Milk: Pour in the coconut milk. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer covered for 30-40 minutes, or until the pork is tender.
  5. Add Chilies and Cream: Add the long green chilies (and Thai chilies if using). Pour in the coconut cream.
  6. Reduce Sauce: Continue simmering, uncovered, until the sauce thickens and the oil separates from the coconut milk.
  7. Season: Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve: Serve hot with rice.

Tools and Equipment

  • Wide pan, wok, or cooking pot
  • Wooden spoon or ladle
  • Chopping board
  • Sharp knife
